The Lean Product Playbook
Review

Overview: This book provides clear, step-by-step guidance to help build successful products. The author provides a framework and process for how to achieve product market fit. The information given is to help contribute to the ideas of the Lean Startup method and describes the Lean Product Process: a repeatable, easy to follow methodology for iterating to product market fit.

Areas of Personal Takeaways:

1) Quantifying prioritization: Learning how to quantify priororities on what to build and who to build for.
2) Metrics and Analysis: If you were to only read a portion of this book then read chapters 13 & 14, in which the author goes into detail on what to measure and what actionable metrics to prioritize. He also explains the analytics and testing of these metrics.
3) Testing for product market fit: Olsen has created an easy to follow framework that helps businesses and startups iterate towards product market fit.

Recommendation:

- Informative: Highly informative. This book sequentially goes through the Lean Agile method and what is needed to be successful. The author hits every main point and explains in detail and with examples.
- Useful: Highly useful. I often come back to this book for reference. The chapters on metrics and analytics were extremely helpful in
- Readability: Moderate.
- Overall: 4.5/5. I absolutely would recommend this book to anyone regardless of their experience. This book thoroughly explains what you need to do, why you need to do it, and how to go about it. I find myself going back to this book for reference quite often. A fast read and definitely worth your time.
