SABRINA MILLER

Hi, thanks for visiting my site!

My name is Sabrina Miller and I am an ambitious APM intern and former student athlete with many years of experience collaborating, competing, and constantly developing as a leader, teammate, and aspiring Product Manager. My perpetual curiosity and passion for technology combined with my experience on leading teams have inspired me to advance my career where I can be challenged, in which I product management has been an excellent fit for my background and prospective development. Working as an APM has given me the exciting opportunity to thrive in an influential and team environment.

I graduated with a degree in Management Information Systems in 2018 and was amidst the top 1% of my major due to my admittance in the Gary J. Sbona Honors Program, where I was the product manager of a deeply technical project sponsored by HPE. Before my current APM internship at Riverfy, I dedicated 2 years to developing my skills at the NASA Ames Research Center as a CR Project Analyst in the Supercomputing Division. In this role, I worked with cross functional teams in the division to build solutions for scientists and researchers to run their code on the supercomputer.

Outside of work, I focus on side projects and products that are geared towards a positive social impact. My first personal project was a Covid-19 dashboard to help update users on what necessities are in stock at popular markets in the area. I also hard coded this portfolio/blog website from scratch while using Lean Agile methodologies. A lot of lessons there! And lastly, I am currently on schedule to launch a BLM Conversational Guide Application by the end of November in the efforts to educate with historical references and unbiased statistics to help build confidence while having these uncomfortable but necessary racial conversations. I am extremely passionate about positive social change and hopefully that shows through my experiences. Product management allows me to bring that unique and diverse perspective given my passions and background while creating inclusive environments and products.

Now that you know me I would love to connect with you! Please feel free to connect with me via any social media with the links in my footer and or my email: sfaith.miller1@gmail.com. Also, any questions, comments, or concerns are not just welcomed but encouraged! Feedback is always a gift and I look forward to hearing from you!
